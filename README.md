### 🚀 Professional Profile
Doctorate-level Mathematician with over 20 years of academic and practical experience. I bridge the gap between rigorous mathematical theory and modern data analytics using **Fedora Linux** environments and **Python**-driven computational workflows.

* 🐧 **Linux & Terminal Mastery:** Expert in advanced text processing (**Grep, Sed, Awk**), system automation, and Bash scripting for large-scale data manipulation.
* 📈 **Freelance Data Analysis:** Specializing in statistical modeling, hypothesis testing, and end-to-end data pipelines for strategic business insights.
* 🎓 **STEM Leadership:** International experience in Mathematics Olympiad coordination and mentoring Arduino-based robotics projects.
